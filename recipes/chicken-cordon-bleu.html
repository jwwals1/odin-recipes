Chicken Cordon Bleu

Classic chicken cordon bleu consists of chicken wrapped around cheese, then breaded and pan-fried or deep-fried.

  • Chicken Breast
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Cooked Ham
  • Bread Crumbs
  1. Pound the chicken.
  2. Place 1 swiss cheese slice and 1 ham slice on top of the chicken.
  3. Roll up the chicken.
  4. Sprikle bread crumbs on the chicken.
  5. Back in the oven for 30 to 35 minutes.
