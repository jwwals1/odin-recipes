Chicken Cordon Bleu

Classic chicken cordon bleu consists of chicken wrapped around cheese, then breaded and pan-fried or deep-fried.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Cooked Ham
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Pound the chicken.

  2. Place 1 cheese slice and 1 ham slice on top of the chicken.

  3. Roll up the chicken.

  4. Sprikle crumbs on the chicken.

  5. Back in the oven for 30 to 35 minutes.
