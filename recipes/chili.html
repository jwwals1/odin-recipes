Chili

Description of chili. The recipe contains beef, tomato sauce, kidney beans, and more.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Tomato Sause
  • Kidney Beans
  • Chili Beans
  • Chili Powder

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.

  2. Stir in kidney beans, chili beans, and tomato sause. Bring to boil and stir in chili powder.
